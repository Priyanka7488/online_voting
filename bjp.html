BHARTIYA JANTA PARTY


The BHARTIYA JANTA PARTY (BJP), translated as the "INDIAN PEOPLE'S PARTY", is one of the prominent parties in India today.It is a major political party with a right wing political position. It is the most significant member of the family of active organisation known as "Sangh Parivar". The official ideology of the BJP is integral humanism, first formulated by Deendayal Upadhaya in 1965. The party expresses a commitment to Hindutva, and its policy has historically reflected Hindu nationalist positions.The BJP advocates social convertism and a foreign policy centred on nationalist principles.

Origin: Bhartiya Jan Sangh

President: Jagat Prakash Nadda

General Secretary: B.L.Santhosh

Founder: Atal Bihari Vajpayee, L.K. Advani

Alliance: NDA(National Democratic Alliance)

Status in Bihar elections:The Bihar Legislative Assembly saw the highest over turnout in Bihar assembly polls since 2000, with a 56.8% voter turnout in this election, BJP won 53 seats, In terms of vote share, Bjp came first with 24.4% followed by RJD with 18.4% and JD(U) with 16.8% and Congress got 6.7%.In 2020 Bihar legislative assebmbly election, the party won 74 seats as a major participant of NDA alliance.
