JANTA DAL UNITED


The JANTA DAL (UNITED), commonly referred as the JD(U), is a regional political party in India with political presence mainly in eastern and north-eastern India.The party was formed with the merger of the Sharad Yadav faction of the Janta Dal, the Lok Shakti and Samta Party.Its political position is center-left operating on the ideologies of integral humanism, secularism and socialism.

President: Lalan Singh

ECI status: Bihar(State party)
Arunachal Pradesh(state party)

Founder: George Fernandes, Nitish Kumar, Sharad Yadav

Headquarter: Jantar Mantar, New Delhi

Founded: 30 OCTOBER 2003

Status in Bihar elections: In 2020 Assembly elections the party performed badly, with its seats reduced to 43 from 75.The breakaway of the faction led by Upendra Kushwaha, who founded Rashtriya Lok Samta Party was recognised as one of the principal reason behind reduction in support base of the party in many constituencies.
