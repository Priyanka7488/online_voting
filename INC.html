INDIAN NATIONAL CONGRESS



The INDIAN NATIONAL CONGRESS is one of the two major political parties in India, along with its main rival the BHARTIYA JANTA PARTY.The Congress is a "big tent" party whose platform is generally considered in the center in its ideological orientation, of Indian politics.

President: Sonia Gandhi

ECI status: National Party

Founder: A.O Hume, W.C. Bonnerjee, Surendranath Banerjee

Founded: 28 Dec 1885

Status in Bihar elections: Congress contested as a part of the Mahaghathbandhan (Grand Alliance) in BIhar legislative assembly election 2020. The Mahaghathbandhan including RJD, Congress and Left parties managed to secure 110 seats in the state's 243 seat legislative assembly in which Congress won19 of 70 seats.
