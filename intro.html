GUIDELINES FOR VOTE CASTING


  1. After Login through Secret Pin. Ypu are Welcome in Instruction page for VOTE CASTING

  2. In VOTE CASTING Page, Your Profile will show, that you are enter personal details in Registration Page. i.e Profile Photo, Name, Aadhar No. , Voter Id , Mobile No.

  3. After entering in Vote Casting Page, You can also see your regional Party No., Party name, Candidate name, Party Symbol and VOTE Button.

  4. See All Party/Candidate then you Can Select any Party/Candidate (Your Individual Choice) then you can Click on Respective VOTE Button.

  5. After Clicking on Respective VOTE Button , you got a Sound (i.e Beep Sound) and Also You got a OTP on E-mail for Successful Vote Casting. If You can't enter OTP for vote casting then Your vote is NOT Counted in Our System.

  6. After Enter a OTP Click on Submit Button.Then your Valuable VOTE is Counted in our System.

  7. After Click On Submit OTP then You are Logout for Vote Casting for respective Election.


    8. Click on "Go For Vote Casting" Button for Vote Casting Page.
